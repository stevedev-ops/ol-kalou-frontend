# DCP Ol Kalou: Official Training Guide
**Complete System Manual for HQ Administrators \u0026 Field Agents**

Welcome to the DCP Ol Kalou Command Center. This guide covers everything you need to know to operate the platform, whether you are managing the campaign from Headquarters or mobilizing voters in the field.

---

## Part 1: Getting Started

### 1.1 How to Access the Platform
Because this is a Progressive Web App (PWA), you do not need to download anything from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
1. Open the secure link provided by your campaign manager on your phone or computer.
2. When prompted by your browser, tap **\\"Add to Home Screen\\"** to install the app directly to your device.

### 1.2 How to Log In
The system uses a frictionless, password-less login to ensure you never get locked out in the field.
1. Open the app and navigate to the Login page.
2. **First Name**: Enter your first name exactly as it appears on your ID.
3. **National ID**: Enter your National ID number.
4. Tap **Log In**. The system will verify your identity and automatically direct you to the correct dashboard (Admin or Agent).

---

## Part 2: Headquarters (HQ) Admin Guide

If you are an HQ Administrator, you have full access to the Command Center and all intelligence data.

### 2.1 The Dashboard \u0026 Analytics
When you log in, you will see the **System Overview**.
* **Total Network**: The total number of verified supporters registered across all wards.
* **Root Mobilizers**: The number of top-level agents directly managed by HQ.
* **System Health**: Indicates if the database is actively syncing with field agents in real time.

### 2.2 Managing Your Network Tree
Navigate to **Hierarchy** or **Root Directory** on the sidebar.
* **Establishing a Root**: Click the \\"Establish Root\\" button at the top. Enter the agent's details. They will act as the top of a new mobilization pyramid with a maximum quota of 25 direct recruits.
* **Viewing Lineage**: Click on any member to view their **Intelligence Profile**. You can see exactly who invited them, how many people they have recruited, and what tier they are on.

### 2.3 Emergency Broadcasts
If you need to send an urgent message to all field agents:
1. Navigate to the **Broadcast** section.
2. Type your message (e.g., \\"URGENT: All agents in Kanjuiri Range report to HQ\\").
3. Click **Send**. This will instantly override the screen of every logged-in agent with your message.

## Part 3: Field Agent \u0026 Mobilizer Guide

If you are a field agent, your job is to recruit supporters and secure votes.

### 3.1 Recruiting New Supporters
1. On your dashboard, locate your **Invite Link** or QR code.
2. Have the supporter scan the code, or fill out the registration form on their behalf.
3. **Quotas**: As a regular mobilizer, you can recruit up to 5 direct supporters. If you are a Root Mobilizer, you can recruit up to 25. Once your quota is full, you cannot recruit more directly.

### 3.2 Voter Verification
When you register a supporter, the system instantly checks the official IEBC voter registry.
* **Green Checkmark (Verified)**: The supporter is a registered voter in the correct ward.
* **Red Cross (Unverified)**: The supporter could not be found in the registry. You must physically verify their voting status.

### 3.3 Field Logistics (Boda-Boda Transport)
If a verified supporter needs transport to the polling station:
1. Navigate to the **Transport** tab.
2. Enter the supporter's pickup location and their assigned polling station.
3. Submit the request. HQ will dispatch a boda-boda rider and update the status in your app when the rider is on the way.

### 3.4 The Panic Button (Incident Reporting)
If you witness voter intimidation, bribery, or violence:
1. Navigate to the **Incidents** tab.
2. Select the type of incident and provide a brief description.
3. Submit. HQ will receive an instant alert with your exact location so they can dispatch legal or security teams.

## Part 4: Election Day Operations (GOTV)

On Election Day, the platform shifts from \\"Recruitment Mode\\" to \\"Tactical Strike Mode\\".

### 4.1 Polling Agent Check-in
If you are an assigned polling agent:
1. Navigate to the **Agents** tab.
2. Click **Check-In** as soon as you arrive at your assigned polling station. HQ monitors this to ensure 100% coverage before polls open.

### 4.2 GOTV Strike-offs (Get Out The Vote)
Your job is to make sure every verified supporter actually votes.
1. Navigate to the **GOTV** tab to see the list of supporters assigned to your polling station.
2. As you see them vote, tap their name to mark them as **\\"Voted\\"**.
3. At 2:00 PM, look at the remaining names on your list. These are the people who have not shown up. Contact them immediately or dispatch a boda-boda to bring them to the station.

### 4.3 PVT (Parallel Vote Tabulation)
Once voting ends and counting begins:
1. Navigate to the **Tally / PVT** tab.
2. Enter the exact number of votes for your candidate, the opponent, and the total valid votes cast.
3. Take a clear, well-lit photo of **Form 34A** and upload it to the system.
4. Hit **Submit**. This feeds directly into HQ's live tally, giving your campaign the results hours before the official announcement and protecting against rigging.
